Ragu Sauce

Description

This meaty ragu is full of flavor and can be used with a variety of pasta shapes. This sauce can be made ahead and frozen for a quick and easy weeknight meal!

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1lb ground pork
  • 2 medium yellow onions, minced
  • 1 shallot, minced
  • 2 large carrots, minced
  • 1 celery stalk, minced
  • 1 6oz can tomato paste
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups red wine
  • 2 cups beef stock
  • 24oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 12oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tsps oregano
  • 2 tsps thyme
  • 2 tsps nutmeg
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • additional water or beef broth as needed
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a stock pot, brown the beef in oil for about 6-7 minutes on high heat. Remove to a bowl.
  2. Repeat with the pork and remove to the same bowl.
  3. Drain some of the excess fat.
  4. Lower the head to medium-high. Add the onions, shallots, carrots, and celery to the pan with more oil if necessary. Saute until soft, about 20 minutes.
  5. Add the garlic and tomato paste, cook until fragrant. About 3 minutes.
  6. Add the meat back to the pot. Add the red wine and deglaze the pan while scraping the bottom.
  7. Add the beef stock, tomatoes, tomato sauce, and spices. Bring to a boil over high heat.
  8. Once boiling, reduce to a simmer. Add the parmesan cheese.
  9. Cook, stirring and skimming fat as needed, for 1.5-2 hours. Taste for seasoning periodically.
